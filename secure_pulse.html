Help Center

Find answers to common questions or contact our support team.

How do I set up my SecurePulse bracelet?

Setting up your SecurePulse is simple. First, download the SecurePulse app from the App Store or Google Play. Turn on your bracelet and make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone. Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your device. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

How do I add emergency contacts?

In the SecurePulse app, go to Settings > Emergency Contacts. Tap the "+" icon and enter the contact information for the people you want to notify in an emergency. You can add multiple contacts and set their priority level.

How long does the battery last?

The SecurePulse battery lasts up to 7 days on a single charge with normal use. Battery life may vary based on usage of GPS and other features. You'll receive a notification in the app when your battery is running low.

Is SecurePulse waterproof?

Yes, SecurePulse has an IP68 waterproof rating, meaning it can withstand immersion in up to 1.5 meters of water for 30 minutes. You can wear it while swimming, showering, or in the rain.

How do I reset my bracelet?

To reset your bracelet, press and hold the side button for 15 seconds until the LED flashes red three times. This will reset the device without erasing your account data. For a complete factory reset, use the "Reset Device" option in the app settings.
